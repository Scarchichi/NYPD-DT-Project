# NYPD Domain Awareness System Impact & Use Policy

## Summary

This official NYPD policy document defines how the Domain Awareness System may be used, including governance, oversight, privacy protections, and operational limits.

Key points:
- The policy is an official NYPD public information document describing permitted uses of DAS data and analytic tools.
- It establishes controls for data retention, access, and sharing.
- The document is part of the department's effort to address civil liberties concerns while maintaining counterterrorism and investigative capabilities.
- It is a source for understanding the NYPD's own stated rules for DAS deployment and use.
