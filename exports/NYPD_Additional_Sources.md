## Timeline of Significant Events
- **2005**: NYPD launches the Real Time Crime Center, an early data warehouse and command center that becomes the foundation for later DAS development.
- **2008**: Lower Manhattan Security Initiative deploys integrated cameras and sensors in the financial district, demonstrating early sensor fusion capabilities.
- **2012**: NYPD and Microsoft publicly announce the citywide rollout of the Domain Awareness System.
- **2014**: DAS expands with broader mobile access and AVL integration for more than 5,000 police vehicles.
- **2016�2017**: Public scrutiny grows as investigative reporting highlights concerns about racial profiling, camera search capabilities, and license plate tracking.
- **2021**: NYPD publishes the Domain Awareness System Impact and Use Policy to define use cases, access controls, and retention limits.
